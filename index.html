Illuminator documentationÔÉÅ

TThe Illuminator is a user-friendly development kit for simulating integrated energy systems. It supports the design of energy systems, the testing of advanced energy management concepts, and the demonstration of challenges posed by the energy transition. Illuminator builts on Mosaik. and supports both stand-alone simulations and distributed computing.
